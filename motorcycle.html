Custom Motorcycle Upholstery

Whether you ride a Harley-Davidson, a vintage cruiser, a sport bike, or a custom chopper, A & E builds motorcycle seats that fit your riding style and your aesthetic.

Foam Work

Custom Foam Work

Because comfort matters on long rides.

The seat shape determines the ride. Our custom foam work lets you dial in the height, firmness, and contour of your seat — whether you want a dropped profile for a slammed look or added density for all-day comfort. We shape, carve, and layer foam by hand to achieve the exact feel you're after, then wrap it in the material of your choice.

All foam work is done in-house by our experienced craftsmen. We don't outsource — every cut, every layer, every contour is handled personally to ensure it meets your requirements before we ever touch the upholstery material.
